    Hi there, my name is Thingler. I am a real-time, collaborative Todo list.

    I was created by designer & developer Alexis Sellier, also known as cloudhead. I am powered by node.js and CouchDB, and was built entirely in JavaScript, using less.js, cradle, journey and node-static. I am running on a Linode instance. You can follow my maker on twitter, and let him know what you think of me. My code is available and open-source—feel free to browse.
